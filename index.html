About This Analysis

How the numbers were assembled

This document represents a complete financial reconciliation of the Girls Groove mobile studio project from first engagement through completion. Every figure has been cross-referenced against primary source records.

Payments received ($139,730.38) were verified deposit by deposit across five years of bank records. Each transaction is listed in the payment ledger with its source, method, and date.

Project costs ($83,688) were compiled from purchase records spanning 2021–2026 — Amazon order history, credit card statements, and subcontractor payments. Every item carries either an order ID, a bank transaction, or both. Subcontractors include electrical, general build, and finish work.

Labor ($99,754) reflects logged project hours at the standard Ignite Multimedia Technical Services billing rate of $150/hr across a five-year engagement encompassing remediation, electrical, systems integration, acoustic design, and project management. Completion work billed at the same rate.

Credit card interest ($6,612) represents the proportional share of financing costs attributable to project purchases, calculated against actual interest charged per statement across both cards used for project procurement.
